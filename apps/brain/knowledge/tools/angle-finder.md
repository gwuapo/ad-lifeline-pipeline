# Angle Finder SOP

## Purpose
Discover untapped ad angles for a product by deeply analyzing the target market, avatars, emotional triggers, and competitive landscape.

## Process

### Step 1: Product Analysis
- What does the product do?
- What is the core mechanism / unique ingredient / differentiator?
- What is the offer (price, guarantee, bonuses)?
- What are the top 3 claims we can make?

### Step 2: Avatar Deep Dive
For each avatar identified:
- Demographics (age, gender, income, location)
- Psychographics (beliefs, values, identity)
- Pain points (specific, not generic -- what keeps them up at night?)
- Desires (what does their ideal outcome look like?)
- Current solutions they've tried and why they failed
- Language they use (forums, Reddit, TikTok comments, Amazon reviews)

### Step 3: Emotional Trigger Mapping
Map each avatar's pain/desire to emotional triggers:
- Fear of missing out
- Fear of judgment
- Desire for status/belonging
- Frustration with current solutions
- Hope for transformation
- Shame/embarrassment
- Curiosity/novelty

### Step 4: Angle Generation
For each angle, provide:
- Angle name (clear, descriptive)
- The emotional trigger it leverages
- A sample hook (first 3 seconds / first line)
- Why this angle would resonate
- Estimated saturation (fresh / moderate / saturated)
- Best platform for this angle (Meta, TikTok, YouTube)

### Step 5: Ranking & Recommendations
- Rank top 10 angles by estimated performance
- Recommend top 3 to test first with reasoning
- Suggest creative format for each (UGC, VSL, static, etc.)

## Quality Standards
- Every angle must be SPECIFIC to this product and avatar -- no generic marketing advice
- Hooks must be scroll-stopping, not clickbait
- Reference the swipe file for proven hook patterns
- Think like a media buyer spending $10K/day -- what would you actually test?
