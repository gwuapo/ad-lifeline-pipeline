# Copywriting Frameworks

## PAS (Problem - Agitate - Solution)
1. **Problem**: Name the specific pain point
2. **Agitate**: Make them feel it. Twist the knife.
3. **Solution**: Present your product as the answer

## AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
1. **Attention**: Hook that stops the scroll
2. **Interest**: Why should they care? Relevance.
3. **Desire**: Paint the transformation. Make them want it.
4. **Action**: Clear CTA with urgency

## BAB (Before - After - Bridge)
1. **Before**: Current painful state
2. **After**: Desired outcome achieved
3. **Bridge**: Your product is the bridge

## 4U Formula (for headlines)
- **Useful**: What benefit does the reader get?
- **Urgent**: Why act now?
- **Ultra-specific**: Use numbers, details
- **Unique**: What makes this different?
